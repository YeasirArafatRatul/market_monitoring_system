CENTRALIZED MARKET MONITORING AND DISTRIBUTION SYSTEM FOR BANGLADESH

Price hike of essential commodities is a “buzz” word which has become the major challenge faced by the people of Bangladesh. Especially, the poorest segment of our country and the limited earners hit hard by the quick movement of price of essential commodities. We see that the main reasons of the problem is syndicate of dishonest traders/distributors. To solve the problem we are going to implement a project called “CENTRALIZED MARKET MONITORING AND DISTRIBUTION SYSTEM FOR BANGLADESH”. In this project we create a transparent distribution flow to ensure fair price in the local markets of each region of the country. We ensure of stop artificial price hikes of daily necessary items. The main aim of this project is to break the syndicate of dishonest traders and ensure rights of consumer.

Features of Our Project



CENTRALIZED
The supply chain of a product can be monitored by a central authority.
TRANSPARENT
All the transactions between traders are visible to the admins.
COMPARABLE
Two layers of market can be compared.

Users

In our System We have 4 different types of users

Importer

  • Can Add Record Of His Imports
  • Can Add Record Of His Sales
  • Can See the availabily of his individual product
  • Can see the average price of individual product in the market

Admin

  • Admin has the control over the whole environment
  • Admin can see all the transactions among traders
  • Admin can see the differecne between the two layers of market
  • Admin can see the status of The Whole Market

Retailer

  • Can Add Record Of His Sales
  • Can See the availabily of his individual product
  • Can see the average price of individual product in the market

Wholesaler

  • Can Add Record Of His Sales
  • Can Send a sell request
  • Can See the availabily of his individual product
  • Can see the average price of individual product in the market
